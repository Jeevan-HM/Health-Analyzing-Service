SHA

Smart Health Analyzing Services is an innovative health monitoring service that leverages advanced technology to analyze your health in real-time. This service provides personalized suggestions and predictions based on your health data to help you maintain your well-being. Using cutting-edge algorithms and machine learning techniques, Smart Health Analyzing Services tracks your vital signs, activities, and provides insightful reports to help you understand your health better. With its intuitive and user-friendly interface, you can easily keep track of your health progress and receive valuable recommendations to achieve your health goals. Smart Health Analyzing Services is the ultimate solution for those who value their health and want to live a happier and healthier life.

Smart Watch

The Smart Watch has sensors that assess physiological data such as heart rate, heart rate variability, respiratory rate, oxygen saturation, wrist temperature, and body mass for health monitoring.

Getting Readings

To monitor patients' health status, healthcare providers can securely gather and handle clinically relevant data such as heart rate, respiration, oxygen saturation, and body mass.

Storing The Readings

Once securely stored in an SQLite database, the collected readings can be analyzed and used to provide web-based recommendations and information..

Analyze The Readings

Health parameter trend analysis can identify abnormal changes, enabling personalized treatment recommendations and actions for proactive health management.

Diet and Workout Recommendations

Customized diet, workout, and medication recommendations can be given based on thorough analysis of health parameters and unique health profile.

Log on Web

A centralized platform offers users access to track their health parameters and relevant information for proactive health management.

Chatbot for Queries

The interactive virtual assistant provides personalized health information, data, suggestions, and assistance to help users achieve their health goals.
